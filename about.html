About Us

Dedicated to transforming lives through personalized nutrition and wellness

Dietitian Bhawana Sachan

Lead Dietitian & Founder

Ms. Bhawana Sachan is one of the most trusted Dieticians, Nutritionists, and Lifestyle Coaches in India with over 15 years of professional experience in nutrition and wellness.

She specializes in developing, monitoring, reviewing, and improving nutritional care plans for clients with diet-related disorders. Her approach emphasizes practical guidance on lifestyle and food choices that can be sustained long-term.

With a deep understanding of how nutrition affects overall health, she focuses on educating people about proper nutrition and helping them address stress and lifestyle-related health concerns through evidence-based dietary interventions.

Many people have accomplished their dietary and lifestyle goals under her direction, transforming their health and quality of life through personalized nutrition plans.
